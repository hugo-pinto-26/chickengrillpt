Company History

Kip Grill were founded by Celso Figueiredo & Miguel Cascalvo.

We share the same goal to become entrepreneurs and it make us think about we could start our own business and the best way was to show our customers the famous traditional Chicken piri piri from Portugal in the meanwhile that is the reason we found Kip Grill.

Food

Mission and Vision

Our vision is to show all our customers the magnificent portuguese cuisine made with charcoal.

Cooking with charcoal and wood gives an amazing flavour on the food, together with a special sauce that bring the chicken to another level.
